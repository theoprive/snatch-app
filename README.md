# Snatch

**Snatch** is a location-based mobile platform designed to help users discover and book real-world experiences happening around them.

The goal of Snatch is to reconnect people with real-life activities by making discovery **simple, spontaneous, and social**.

Instead of scrolling endlessly through social media, users open Snatch to quickly find experiences, events, and activities happening nearby.

---

## Product Vision

Modern digital platforms often disconnect people from real-world interactions.  
Snatch aims to reverse that trend by making **offline experiences discoverable in a social, intuitive interface**.

The platform connects two types of users:

• **Snatchers** – people discovering and joining experiences  
• **Experience creators** – organizers sharing activities with their community

The product is designed to make spontaneous participation easy and engaging.

---

## Key Features

- **Experience Feed**  
  A discovery feed inspired by social platforms that surfaces nearby experiences.

- **Location-Based Discovery**  
  Experiences appear based on user location and contextual signals.

- **User Profiles**  
  Users create profiles and manage their experience history.

- **Messaging & Connections**  
  Users can connect and communicate with other participants.

- **Experience Details & Booking Flow**  
  Dedicated pages for each experience with key information.

- **Community-Based Experiences**  
  Campus or local communities can host and promote activities.
